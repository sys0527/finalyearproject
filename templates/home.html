Scope & Aim

Scope

Increase the interactivity and smoothness of the lecturer classes at a minimized cost.

Aim

Enables lecturers to control the mouse through hand gesture.

Functions

Action 1 : Move

Raise your index finger and move the cursor according to the position of your fingertip.
The red box shows an equal scale reduction of the actual size of the screen.

Action 2 : Left Click & Right Click

Left Click : raise the index finger + middle finger
Right Click : raise the thumb + index finger + middle finger

Close the index and middle finger to click.

Action 3 : Scroll Up & Down

Scroll Up : raise 4 finger
Scroll Down : raise 4 finger and folded

Action 4 : Drag

Raise your thumb and index finger, move the cursor with your index fingertip.
The red box shows an equal scale reduction of the actual size of the screen.

Action 5 : Screenshot

Do a 'ok' hand action, fold the last three fingers for 1 second to capture the currnet view of screen.
